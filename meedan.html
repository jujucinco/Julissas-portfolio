About the project

I'm currently a UX Designer at Meedan, where I help build features for Check, a web based app for media newsrooms and content moderation teams.

The primary purpose of Check is to help these teams engage with communities on closed messaging networks such as WhatsApp and Telegram to help fight misinformation by providing factual, source backed reports via their tiplines


My role

While collaborating alongside the Director of Product, engineers and the customer support team, I'm responsible for the end to end process for feature improvements on Check. This begins with conducting regular formative research and testing with users from large social/news media organizations to learn about their painpoints, and finishes with handing off polished prototypes and detailed specs to engineers.

Accomplishments

  • Initiated new team processes such as user feedback and testing at more regular intervals and bi-weekly product team design feedback meetings
  • Built research repository to track feedback and feature requests
  • Contributed to building a design system from scratch
  • Facilitated workshops for other teams using a design sprint approach and using collaboration tools such as FigJam.
  • Responsible for countless feature additions and improvements, with others ready for dev
